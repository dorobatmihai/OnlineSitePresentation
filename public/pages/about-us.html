Frequently Asked Questions

Products Choice

Q:

Do I need help choosing the right products?

A: We are happy to advise you on the choice of nutritional supplements, fitness foods and other products that you find on our website. It is enough to contact the customer relations service at the number 0040 316 302 838 or by e-mail at the address info@gymbeam.ro

Q:

How can I find out information about product availability?

A: Information about product availability can be obtained by sending a e-mail at info@gymbeam.ro

Q:

What does the "basic price" displayed next to the products mean?

A: The normal price refers to the average value, at which you can purchase the product from other online stores. GymBeam guarantees you the best prices.

Q:

Where can I find the size chart?

A: The sizes of the garment are generally indicated in table of sizes, present in the product description, at the bottom of the page. If you haven't found the requested information, please call the service relations with customers, on the phone number 0040 316 302 838 or contact us by e-mail at address info@gymbeam.ro

Order registration and processing

Q:

Do I have to have an account when I want to place an order?

A: No, just fill out the order form. However, it is useful to have a registered account, because with with the help of the customer account, you will be able to monitor the status order.

Q:

Why can't I access my account?

A: You may have entered a wrong password or you may be registered with a different email address. If you have problems to access your account, please contact us by email to the address info@gymbeam.ro or by phone at the number 0040 316 302 838.

Q:

Can the products be ordered by phone?

A: If you cannot place an order on our website www.gymbeam.ro, please contact us by phone at number 0040 316 302 838 or send us a e-mail at info@gymbeam.ro and we can offer you support in its placement.

Delivery and payment of products

Q:

When will I receive the package?

A: Parcels are delivered within approximately 24 hours from placing the order. Contracted courier companies guarantees the delivery of the order in the shortest possible time. In the during public holidays or during the campaigns of discounts, we cannot guarantee parcel delivery within 24 hours. During the aforementioned periods, the delivery of parcels it depends on the schedule of the courier companies and the capacity their delivery.

Q:

Do you also deliver orders on weekends?

A: Unfortunately, this service is not available.

Q:

Where can I track the status of my package?

A: After shipping the package from our warehouse, you will receive an informative e-mail with the shipment number - AWB. Afterwards, you can track the order status directly on the website the selected courier company.

Q:

How to use the voucher / discount coupon?

A: The coupon or gift voucher can be used in the first order step. At the bottom, above the button "Complete the order by choosing the payment method", there is a box where you can enter the coupon number of reduction. Don't forget to confirm by pressing the Apply button discount code (arrow). The system will deduce the value gift voucher from your order.

Q:

What are the payment options?

A: Payment on delivery – The parcel can be paid in cash to the courier, at the time of its collection at the address mentioned by you. Administrative fee for payment on delivery is 5 RON.

Online payment with bank card - You can pay the order, using the bank card. It is an affordable and safe method of payment. We currently accept online card payments VISA, VISA Electron, Maestro and MasterCard banking.

Bank transfer – You can pay for products and through online banking services. At the address of e-mail you will receive all the necessary information to make the payment by bank. You can make the payment within 3 working days from placing the order. Don't forget to fill all required data otherwise the process can be extended. Products will be sent only after the payment has been registered in our account.

GymBeam gift voucher – the gift voucher for shopping can be applied to the entire range of products present on the website www.gymbeam.ro. Enter the code gift voucher in the "Apply discount code" box, which appears in the first step of placing the order. The value of the gift voucher will be deducted from the order total. In the within an order you can only use one voucher.
